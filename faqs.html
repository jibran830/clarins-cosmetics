Frequently Asked Questions

Are your products cruelty-free?

Yes, all our products are 100% cruelty-free and not tested on animals.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping typically takes 3–7 business days, depending on your location. Expedited options are also available at checkout.

Can I modify or cancel my order after placing it?

You can modify or cancel your order within 1 hour of purchase. After that, the order is processed and cannot be changed

What’s the shelf life of your products?

Most products have a shelf life of 12–24 months. Check the packaging for specific expiration dates.
